Who Am I?

I am a second year student at Coventry University. Over the last 5 years, I have been volunteering my time to help raise money or awareness for many different charities; including the likes of Islamic Relief, SKT, WE Day and also Fisher House. Each charity represents something different, such as; helping to raise money to clothe unfortunate children in third world countries, to raise money for injured soldiers and helping their families, to raise money for books and pens to provide unfortunate children an education. I had the chance to support families who were going through difficult times. I developed the ability to communicate sensitively and effectively with children and adults supporting them in difficult circumstances. I am self-motivated and have enhanced my communication skills through volunteering, which has also improved my confidence in interacting with new people. This experience gave me the most important skill which is to have a non-judgmental approach which therefore gave me the ability to build good relationships with families.
